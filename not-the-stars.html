I


‍ Not the stars now shine
Not the planets align
The innate gloom and despair
Peeks through every single line

Even when out of sight
Well within the times of light

I choose the dimmer littler me that doesnt shine as bright
That doesnt repel, starts no fight,
And slowly leaves she who knows what is right

Remains the only version I see as me
Remains the sad words that I choose to be

Watering down the truths the depths of emotion
Rendering useless every attempt to grasp at a notion
Im moving forward faster than I can track
Just to prove that I am rid of all devotion

But am I?

Becoming a facade of despair
The illusion of one half of one pair

So that even if all the stars shine
And all of the planets align
The so called innate gloom and despair
Will still be visible in every single line

I still won’t be comfortable with being “fine”
I will mistake for fault, what I should just take as being mine

Because I took things for granted once and now not stars shine
And not even any of the planets align
Now there is real gloom and despair
That this one half of one pair
Expresses through every single line
Now nevermind comfortable, I’m horrified of being “fine”
